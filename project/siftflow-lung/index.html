Lung and Heart segmentation from Chest X-ray using SIFTflow Algorithm

 

In this project, I reproduced the results of a recently published paper on Cardiomegaly detection using SIFTFlow by Candemir et. al. The codes and results are not yet made public as they are a part of an ongoing study.
